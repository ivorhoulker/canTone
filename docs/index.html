啱音字 canTone

A fast, local, cross-platform tool for Cantonese lyricists

Download for iOS
iOS · macOS · Windows

Why 啱音字?

  • • 100% Local & Reliable: Runs locally on your machine. No server crashes, no lag, works completely offline.
  • • Instant Search: Native database processes multi-syllable queries in real-time as you type.
  • • Customizable: Tailor the interface to your workflow. Toggle Jyutping, frequencies, and melodies.

About the Creator & Support

啱音字 is made by Ivor Houlker (艾浩家). I'm an occasional programmer and composer, and my Cantonese isn't good enough to always hear when lyrics don't quite fit. I'm trying my best, but if there are any linguistic inaccuracies or bugs, I'm sorry.
